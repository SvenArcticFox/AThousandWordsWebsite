About Me

My name is Jody Radez

Why picture framing? I have been working with my hands and creative designing for over 50 years. Whether as a musician, woodworker, or renovation designer, I've gained much satisfaction from presenting a finished product that is appreciated. The available time that retirement offers is an opportunity to participate in the visual arts.

I studied this craft with Frank Miragliotta who is a well known and respected picture framer of over 40 years in Long Island.
